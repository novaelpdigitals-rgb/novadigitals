Video Content Creation

We produce fully animated, live-action, and hybrid instructional videos for every subject and grade level — Primary 1 through SS3. Our videos are engaging, curriculum-aligned, and teacher-reviewed before delivery.

Every video follows our proven 4-part learning structure: Hook, Instruction, Practice, and Review — ensuring students retain what they watch.

  • Fully animated explainer videos (2D & 3D)
  • Live-action classroom-style recordings
  • Whiteboard and motion graphics videos
  • Bilingual options (English + major local languages)
  • Teacher guide videos and lesson intros
  • Curriculum-aligned across WAEC, NECO, IGCSE standards
  • Download + streaming versions for both online/offline schools
