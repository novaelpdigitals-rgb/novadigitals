About Novaelp

We Believe Every Child Deserves Great Content

Founded by educators and storytellers, Novaelp bridges the gap between curriculum and engagement through the power of video and digital media.

Our Story

Built from the Classroom, for the Classroom

Novaelp was founded in 2019 by a team of educators who were frustrated watching students disengage from learning materials that hadn't evolved in decades. We set out to fix that.

Today, we work with schools, libraries, and resource centers across Nigeria and beyond, producing instructional videos and digital learning materials that teachers trust and students actually enjoy.

Our content is curriculum-aligned, culturally relevant, and designed with the latest educational research in mind — not just as videos, but as experiences that stick.

🎯 Our Mission

To make world-class educational content accessible to every school, regardless of size or budget.

🌟 Our Vision

A future where every student in Africa has access to engaging, modern instructional media.

💡 Innovation

We constantly evolve our production techniques to stay ahead of how students learn today.

🤝 Partnership

We work hand-in-hand with educators to ensure every piece of content actually serves learning.
