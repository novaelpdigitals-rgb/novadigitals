About Our Content

What types of educational content does Novaelp produce? +
Novaelp produces animated instructional videos, live-action teaching videos, whiteboard explainer videos, digital workbooks, interactive PDFs, e-books, assessment tools, and lesson plan templates. Our content covers all core school subjects from Primary 1 through SS3, and is aligned with national curricula including WAEC and NECO standards.
Is your content aligned with the Nigerian curriculum? +
Yes. All our content is developed in alignment with the national curriculum approved by the Nigerian Educational Research and Development Council (NERDC), as well as WAEC and NECO syllabus requirements. We work with certified teachers and curriculum specialists to review every piece of content before release.
Can you produce content in local Nigerian languages? +
Yes! We offer bilingual and localized content production in Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, and other major Nigerian languages as part of our custom project service. This is available on the Premium plan or as a custom project add-on. Contact us to discuss your language requirements.
How do I know the quality is good before purchasing? +
We offer a 2-week free trial of our School plan, which gives you and your teachers access to sample content across multiple subjects. You can also book a demo call where we'll walk you through our full content library and answer any questions about quality and fit for your school.

Pricing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept bank transfers (Nigerian and international), Paystack, Flutterwave, and invoiced payments for institutional purchases. For large institutional orders, we can issue a proforma invoice for purchase order processing.
Can I pay in installments? +
Yes — we offer termly payment plans for all annual subscriptions. Under this arrangement, you can pay in three installments (once per school term). A small administrative fee applies. Contact us when requesting your quote to set up a termly payment plan.
Is there a refund policy? +
For library subscriptions, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the content quality within the first 30 days, we'll issue a full refund. For custom production projects, refunds are governed by the specific project contract terms agreed upon at the start of the project.

Delivery & Access

How is content delivered to our school? +
We offer two delivery methods: (1) USB Drive — we ship a preloaded USB drive to your school address, perfect for schools with limited internet connectivity. (2) Online Streaming Portal — your school gets login credentials to our web-based content platform, accessible from any device with internet. Premium subscribers get both options included.
Does our school need internet to access the content? +
No! All our plans include USB offline delivery, so your school can use the content without an internet connection. The online portal is an additional option for schools that prefer streaming. We understand that internet connectivity can be unreliable in many parts of Nigeria, which is why we prioritize offline-first delivery.
How often is new content added? +
We add new videos and materials every month. School and Premium subscribers receive these updates automatically — for online subscribers via the portal, and for USB subscribers via a quarterly updated USB drive. We also release seasonal content (revision packs, exam prep materials) ahead of WAEC and NECO examination periods.
How long does a custom project take? +
Turnaround time depends on the scope. A single instructional video (5–10 minutes) typically takes 3–4 weeks from briefing to delivery. A multi-episode series or complete digital workbook package may take 8–16 weeks. We'll provide a detailed production timeline in your project proposal. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
