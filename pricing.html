Common Questions About Pricing

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. We'll prorate the difference for your remaining subscription period.

Is there a payment plan available?

Yes — we offer termly payment options for all plans. Contact us to arrange a flexible payment schedule.

Do prices include VAT?

All prices listed are exclusive of VAT. A 7.5% VAT will be applied to invoices for Nigerian institutions.

Can I get a free trial?

Yes — we offer a 2-week free trial of our School plan. Contact us to get your trial access set up.
